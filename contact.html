Request an invite by email

untitledteamsupport@gmail.com

Fill this out and the site will open your email app with a message already written to untitledteamsupport@gmail.com. The website itself does not save or send anything.

After you press Create Email Request, check your email app before sending.

What to include

  • Your Discord username or display name.
  • What role you want: dev, art, animation, voice acting, music, testing, writing, or community help.
  • A short reason for joining so the owner can tell real requests apart from spam.

No public invite means random people are less likely to join just to cause problems.

Need help?

Email the team if you need project info, game media help, or support. Clear messages are easier to answer.

Want to join?

Developers, animators, voice actors, artists, music composers, testers, writers, and community helpers can all be useful. Open roles can change.
