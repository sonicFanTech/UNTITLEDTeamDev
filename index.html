About

What this team is about

Small team, big character energy

UNTITLED TEAM is built around people jumping in where they are good: coding, animating, drawing, voice acting, writing music, testing ideas, and helping projects feel alive.

The style is bright, chaotic, and mascot-heavy, but the goal is simple: make fun stuff, show progress, and give the community a place to follow along.
