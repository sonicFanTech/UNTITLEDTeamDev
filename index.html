Who they are

Creative games with a team-first feel

UNTITLED TEAM is built around people with different roles working together: developers, animators, voice actors, artists, and music creators. The team has a strong Discord community presence and a style that mixes bright character art with darker mascot-like visuals.

Their projects lean into bold character energy, chaotic creativity, and a mix of bright community art with darker mascot-style visuals.
